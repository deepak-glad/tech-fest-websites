B Plan

The job marketplace has never been more welcoming for entrepreneurs who want to work for themselves. You don’t need a master’s degree, a thick portfolio, or an impressive resume to excel at entrepreneurship. The things you need most are your presentation skills and your ability to tackle problems head-on.So Yantragene brings you a new event, B-plan. In this event participants will be required to form teams of 3-5 members. In the first round, the participants will present their idea with a PowerPoint presentation, those who qualify will move to the next round which is the Q&A round. Marks will be awarded based on the presentation skills and originality among other things. It's time for some entrepreneurship!
