Robo Ballon Fight

Think of the robots as charioteer fighters, sworn to protect the balloons because their lives depend on it. Well, that is pretty much Robo Balloon Fight. This event, at Yantragene, is new but nonetheless fun and exciting to a different level. Participants will need to create a wireless robot that has a needle on the front side and the balloon pasted on it’s back. To win, the participants will have to pop their opponent’s balloons. There will be 3 rounds in a match. The team which wins 2 shall be announced the flawless victor of the game. So get your warships ready.
