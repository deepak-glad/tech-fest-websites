Robo Soccer

This country’s obsession with soccer is no secret. It’s an explosive sport that binds us all. But we, at Yantragene, are taking it a step further. We’re replacing the sportsmen with the BOTS created by our technocrats. But, most importantly we’re NOT dialing down on the soccer spirit and enthusiasm. Electronic enthusiasts can now prove their soccer skills, without their physical strength, but through guiding their robots to score goals for them. It’s a game of teamwork, leadership, and most importantly, heart. So put on your soccer shoes and get your bots ready.
