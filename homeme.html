ABOUT US

Yantragene is the annual science and technology fest of the Feroze Gandhi Institute Of Engineering and Technology Raebareli. Started in 2015 with the aim of providing a platform for the student community to develop and showcase their technical prowess, a grand two-day event on the campus of FGIET Raebareli attracting people from all walks of life including students, academics, professionals and the general public. Year after year, Techfest explores the various aspects of science and technology and the profound impacts they have on our lives. For the last 5 years, Yantragene has constantly grown, evolved, and pushed the boundaries of what a college fest can be. With something to offer for everyone, Yantragene is the ideal destination for all technophiles: young or beginner or expert, student or professional to witness, learn and experience the wonders of science and technology. This year marks the 5th Edition of Yantragene, FGIET Raebareli. The fest shall take place from coming soon 2020 at the beautiful, green campus of FGIET Raebareli, India. Boasting of a huge roster of exciting and engaging events, this edition of Techfest promises to be grander, greater and more glorious than ever before. So grab your friends, mark your calendars, and gear up for Yantragene Science and Technology Fest. Welcome to Yantragene 2020.
