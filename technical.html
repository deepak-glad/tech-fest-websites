Technical Paper Presentation

This is a fairly new event at Yantragene. In this event, participants will present their research in the form of a technical paper that follows IEEE paper submission guidelines. The participants will express their views regarding a particular topic by their research and communicating with attention towards the audience. The event will be conducted in two rounds. The first round is the "Paper Submission" round and the second round is the "PowerPoint presentation" round. Marks will be awarded based on the credibility and originality of the paper and the speaking skills of the participants. It's time for some research that'll blow everyone away!
