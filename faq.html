Basic

  • When is next edition of Yantragene?
    Yantragene 2020 will be held from 26th to 27th March. Gear up for the 2 most awesome days of 2020.
  • How can I paricipate in Yantragene'20?
    To participate in Yantragene'20 you need to register first. For each event, you want to participate you need to register separately.
  • How can I be allowed to participate at a given event venue?
    Each participant must show his/her "Identity Card " which you have received from the "Registration Desk ". This ID card has a unique number which is required before you participate in your event. Also, the event you want to take part in should be mentioned on your ID card by the coordinator at the Registration Desk..
  • What if I do not have a Registration ID before coming to the festival?
    Registration ID well before the start of your interested event. But doing this ensures that you are opting for "On-Spot Registration " which does not guarantee whether you will be getting a slot to participate or not. It is strongly recommended that you register only before coming to the festival to assure your participation.

Accomodation

  • What type of accomodation will be providied?
    Accommodations will be provided to boys and girls in well-secured separate residential complexes on the campus of FGIET.
  • Few members of my team will be arriving late,what to do about their accomodation?
    No issues! You can take accommodation for those members as and when they arrive, you can book accommodation for them beforehand.
  • Are there ATMs available inside?
    Yes, ATMs are available.
  • We are a group of friends not participating in any of the competitions and just coming to have fun. Would we be allowed?
    No, but you can contact Registeration Desk and request for accommodation.

Others

  • How to find a way to any venue of the event?
    To find the location and direction of any venue you can access the “Sign board” or just google it on Maps.
  • What is the artist’s line-up?
    Stay tuned to our Official Facebook page.
  • Who should I contact in case of an emergency during yantragene?
    The emergency Contact number will be given on the registration booklet.
  • Should I carry some ID proof every time?
    You should carry your ID card as well as your registration booklet all the time, As a security check is regularly done.
